NI-YU TEX INTERNATIONAL is a trusted apparel buying house and supply chain partner headquartered in Bangalore, India. Manufacturing

We operate our own dedicated denim manufacturing facility covering pattern making, sampling, cutting, stitching, washing, finishing, quality inspection, and packing. All other product categories are produced through our carefully selected, certified vendor network across India, Bangladesh, China, and the Middle East.
