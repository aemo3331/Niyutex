About Us

A trusted apparel buying house & supply chain partner

Headquartered in Bangalore, India, NI-YU TEX INTERNATIONAL partners with fashion brands, retailers, wholesalers, and private labels worldwide — delivering complete, end-to-end apparel solutions built on quality, ethics, and reliability.
